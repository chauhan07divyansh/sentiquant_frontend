## Welcome to Sentiquant

Welcome to Sentiquant, your AI-powered companion for stock market analysis. In this guide, we'll walk you through everything you need to know to start making data-driven trading decisions.

## What is Sentiquant?

Sentiquant is an advanced AI platform that analyzes **250+ NSE & BSE stocks** in real-time, providing you with:

- **AI-generated signals** (BUY/SELL/HOLD)
- **Price targets** (T1, T2, T3)
- **Stop-loss recommendations**
- **Risk assessment scores**
- **Market sentiment analysis**

## How It Works

Our AI models process thousands of data sources including technical indicators, news sentiment, social media trends, historical price patterns, and market fundamentals — synthesising them into a single, actionable output in under 60 seconds.

### Step-by-Step Guide

#### 1. Browse Stocks

Start by exploring our stock list at `/stocks`. You'll find 250+ stocks organized by sector and market cap.

#### 2. View AI Analysis

Click on any stock to see detailed AI analysis including:

- Current price and trend direction
- BUY/SELL/HOLD signal with overall score out of 100
- Three graduated price targets
- Recommended stop-loss level
- Investment grade (A+ to D)

#### 3. Choose Your Time Horizon

Sentiquant supports two distinct trading approaches:

- **Swing Analysis** — for 1 to 4 week trades based on technical momentum
- **Position Analysis** — for 6 to 18 month holds based on fundamental trajectory

#### 4. Build Your Portfolio

Use our portfolio builder to:

- Set your investment budget
- Choose risk appetite (Low/Medium/High)
- Select time horizon
- Get AI-optimised stock allocation with position sizes

## Key Features

### Real-Time Analysis

Our AI fetches live NSE and BSE price data on every request — no cached or stale values. Every analysis reflects current market conditions.

### Investment Grade System

Every stock receives a letter grade from **A+ (Excellent)** to **D (Poor)** based on a composite score across technicals, fundamentals, and sentiment.

### Side-by-Side Comparison

Use the **Compare tab** on any stock to see both Swing and Position analyses simultaneously — helping you choose the right strategy for your goals.

## Pro Tips for New Users

> **Tip 1:** Always respect the stop-loss recommendation. It marks the price where the original trade thesis breaks down.

> **Tip 2:** Use the investment grade as a quick filter — focus on A and B grade stocks for your first trades.

> **Tip 3:** Diversify across sectors. The portfolio builder handles this automatically based on your risk profile.

## Getting Started Checklist

1. Sign up for a free account at Sentiquant
2. Browse the stock list and explore a few stocks you know
3. Run a swing analysis on one stock — review the signal, targets, and stop-loss
4. Try the portfolio builder with your available capital
5. Use the Compare tab to see both trading modes side-by-side
